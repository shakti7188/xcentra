# Implementation Prompt for Cloude Code: Xcentra Website Overhaul

**Objective:** Transform the current Xcentra website from a freelancer-centric landing page into a comprehensive, content-rich, and diversified global finance platform.

---

## 1. Audience Diversification & Messaging Shift
**Current Issue:** The site over-indexes on "Freelancers" and "Remote Workers."
**Task:** Update all headlines and body text to cater to a broader "Global Citizen" audience. Use the following segments:
*   **International Travelers:** Focus on "Zero FX markups" and "Spend like a local."
*   **Expats & Global Families:** Focus on "Sending money home" and "Managing multi-currency life."
*   **Tech-Savvy Shoppers:** Focus on "Secure online subscriptions" and "Privacy-first spending."
*   **Crypto-Native Savers:** Focus on "Real-world utility for stablecoins" without the need for manual off-ramping.

**Specific Text Changes:**
*   **Hero Section:** Change "Built for freelancers, remote workers..." to "Built for global travelers, expats, and the modern digital economy."
*   **Use Cases:** Replace the "Global Freelancer" focus with a "Global Lifestyle" grid featuring:
    1. **Travel:** Pay for hotels and dining in 100+ countries.
    2. **Shopping:** Securely pay for global subscriptions (Netflix, AWS, Spotify).
    3. **Family:** Send stablecoins to family members who can spend them instantly via their own Xcentra card.
